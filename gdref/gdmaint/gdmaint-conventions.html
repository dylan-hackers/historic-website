NOTE: None of the advice contained in this chapter is binding. However, an attempt has been made to accurately represent the working style and informal rules followed by the current developers.

If you think that this chapter does not represent the way we work, please tell me and I'll fix it. It's a “descriptive” document, not “prescriptive” one.

Gwydion Dylan was originally developed by the Gwydion Project at CMU. Today, it is maintained by a loose group of volunteers, some of whom have been working on it for over a year. This chapter attempts to summarize the way we do things.
