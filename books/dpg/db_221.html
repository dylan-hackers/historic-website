13.9.4 The angle library

At this point, we are ready to define the angle library, which will share the sixty-unit and say libraries with the time library. In Chapter 14, Four Complete Libraries, we present the consolidated changes to the sixty-unit, say, and time libraries that we have developed in this chapter, followed by the complete definition of the angle library.
